Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 27, 2025

This privacy policy explains how your information is handled by the RecipeMatch mobile application.

1. No Server-Side Data Collection

The app is designed to be completely **serverless**. This means we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, usage data, or recipe preferences to any remote servers. All of your activity, including recipe swipes and saved items, is stored **locally on your device only**.

2. Information Stored Locally

The app stores data on your device to function properly. This includes:

  • Recipe Preferences: Records of recipes you have swiped right on ("liked") or swiped left on ("disliked") to improve recipe recommendations.
  • Saved Recipes: The list of recipes you have added to your cart for future reference.

This information is not accessible to us or any third parties. If you delete the app from your device, this local data will be permanently erased.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
